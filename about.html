ABOUT US

Who are Okoa Mtaa Foundation?


   Okoa Mtaa Foundation is one of the few organizations in Tanzania that uses art and creativity as a means of simplifying and spreading social education. Through art, we convey knowledge and other social-economic information to the community while celebrating the youth's creativity.

    We engage with large groups of young people, who are not reached by many media/programs in Tanzania.We also aim to achieve Sustainable Development Goals. We chose five goals from the list, which are No Poverty (1), Quality Education (4), Decent work and economic growth (8), Peace, justice, and strong institutions (16), and Partnership for the goals (17). We aim to make sure the youth own and live SDGs in a very creative and participatory way.

    We are passionate about reaching young men and women with and through arts. We provide artistic knowledge and creative safe platforms for underground artists. We strongly believe in a progressive Tanzania society, where arts and culture promote freedom of expression and social education.

Our Vision

 A progressive society that uses Arts, culture, and creativity for social-economic development:

Creative youth who recognize the value of their talents and respect their culture. By Dedicating their creativity and imagination to live and accomplish their dreams for personal benefit and the National as a whole. 

Our Mission

To use underground arts for Social Education:

To advocate for the arts to be among the official learning method for schools & universities in Tanzania, and Uplift underground artists to be recognized as "artivists" and development gatekeepers for their contributions to Community empowerment .
