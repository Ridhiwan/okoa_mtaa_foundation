MAKOROKORO

   Makorokoro is a creative environmental project focusing on four waste management decisions: reduce, reuse, recycle and repurpose. People come together to sell/exchange what they don’t need anymore. For years has been a lovely technique to help people protect their environment indirectly and celebrate with family. This event is also famous as the "Garage sale"
