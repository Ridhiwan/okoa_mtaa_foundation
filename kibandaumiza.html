KIBANDA UMIZA

   "Kibanda Umiza" is an original Swahili Slang that stands for "Slum Movie Theater". This project uses educational films and drama to spreading knowledge and important message to a specific society. This method empowers the community to discuss their issues without pointing fingers at each other, as they feel comfortable discussing challenges in a film that is related to their real issues, and they find solutions together.
